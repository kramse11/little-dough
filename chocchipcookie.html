üç™ Chocolate Chip Cookies

Prep: 15 min | Cook: 13 min | Servings: 10-12 | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
All-purpose flour 2 1/4 cups
Baking soda 1 tsp
Cornstarch 1 1/2 tsp
Salt 1/2 tsp
Unsalted butter, melted 3/4 cup
Brown Sugar 3/4 cup
Granulated Sugar 1/2 cup
Vanilla Extract 2 tsp
Chocolate Chips 1 cup
Eggs 1 egg and 1 egg yolk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C).
  2. Cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add egg, egg yolk and vanilla extract and mix until combined.
  4. Mix in flour, baking soda, cornstarch, and salt.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by spoonfuls onto a baking sheet.
  7. Bake 13 to 15 minutes until edges are set and slightly golden brown.

Note: Cookies will look slightly underbaked but will continue to set as they cool on the baking sheet.
