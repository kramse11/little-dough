Prep: 2 hour | Cook: 25 min | Servings: 8 | Difficulty: Moderate

Ingredients

Ingredients Amount
Warm water 1 1/2 cups
instant or active dry yeast 2 3/4 tsp
Suagr, granulated or brown 1 tbsp
All-purpose or bread flour 4 cups
Salt 2 tsp
Non-stick spray, butter, or oil For coating the bowl
Pot of water 2 Quarts
Honey 1/2 cup
Egg wash 1 egg white

Instructions

  1. Create a warm water mixture with yeast and sugar and let sit until foamy, about 5-10 minutes.
  2. Add the warm water mixture to the flour and salt and mix until a dry, shaggy dough forms.
  3. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and knead for 5-7 minutes until smooth.
  4. Place dough in a lightly oiled bowl. Cover with a towel and let rise for 1 hour.
  5. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
  6. Once dough has risen, punch down and divide into 8 equal pieces. Shape each piece into a ball and press a hole in the center and stretch to 1-2inches in diameter.
  7. Arrange shaped bagels on the prepared baking sheets.
  8. Preheat oven to 425¬∞F (220¬∞C).
  9. Fill a pot with 2 quarts of water and honey and bring to a boil. Drop 2-3 bagels at a time into the pot and cook for 1 minute on each side.
  10. Place bagels on the prepared baking sheets and brush with egg wash and top with desired toppings.
  11. Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.

Note: When kneading, you'll know the dough is ready when it's smooth and elastic. Gently press the dough with your finger; if it springs back, it's ready.
