ü•Ø Cinnamon Rolls

Prep: 2 hours | Cook: 27-30 min | Servings: 6-8 | Difficulty: Moderate

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
All-purpose flour 4 1/8 cups
Granulated sugar 3/8 cup
Salt 3/4 tsp
Warm Milk 1 1/8 cup
Unsalted butter, melted 4 1/2 tbsp
instant or active dry yeast 3 3/8 tsp
Eggs 2 eggs
For Filling Amount
Brown Sugar 1/2 cup
Cinnamon 2 tbsp
Unsalted Butter, softened 3 tbsp
For Icing Amount
Cream Cheese 4 ounces
Unsalted Butter, softened 2 tbsp
Confectioners' Sugar 2/3 cup
Vanilla Extract 1 tsp

Instructions

  1. Combine warm milk, butter, and yeast in a large bowl. Let stand until foamy, about 5-10 minutes.
  2. Add in eggs and all dry ingredients.
  3. Mix until it forms a dry, shaggy dough.
  4. Transfer dough to a lightly floured surface and knead until smooth and elastic, about 5-7 minutes.
  5. Form into a ball and let rest for 10 minutes while you prepare the filling.
  6. Combine brown sugar, cinnamon, and softened butter in a small bowl.
  7. Roll dough into a 12x18 inch rectangle.
  8. Spread filling over dough.
  9. Roll up from long side and cut into 6-8 pieces. You can make them smaller if you prefer.
  10. Place rolls in greased 9x13 inch pan and let rise until doubled in size, about 60-90 minutes.
  11. Preheat oven to 375¬∞F (190¬∞C).
  12. Bake 27-30minutes until golden brown.
  13. For the icing, combine softened cream cheese and butter in a small bowl and using a mixer, cream until smooth.
  14. Sift in confectioners' sugar and vanilla extract until smooth.

Note: If the cinnamon rolls are browning too quickly, cover them with foil to slow the browning process.

Note: Sifting the confectioners' sugar helps prevent lumps in the icing.
