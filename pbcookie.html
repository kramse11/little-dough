ü•ú Peanut Butter Cookies

Prep: 1 hour | Cook: 11 min | Servings: 10-12 | Difficulty: Easy

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
All-purpose flour 1 cup
Baking soda 1/2 tsp
Cream of tartar 1/2 tsp
Salt 1/2 tsp
Unsalted butter, softened 1/2 cup
Brown Sugar 3/4 cup
Granulated Sugar 1/4 cup
Vanilla Extract 2 tsp
Peanut Butter 1/2 cup
Egg 1 egg

Instructions

  1. Cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  2. Add egg and vanilla extract and mix until combined.
  3. Mix in flour, baking soda, cream of tartar, and salt.
  4. Mix in peanut butter.
  5. Cover dough and chill for at least 1 hour.
  6. Preheat oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C).
  7. Spoon dough balls onto a baking sheet.
  8. Use a fork to flatten each cookie and create a crisscross pattern.
  9. Bake 11-12 minutes until edges are set and slightly golden brown.

Note: You can make these into Snickerdoodles by rolling the dough in cinnamon sugar before baking and taking out the peanut butter. Or just make them into peanut butter snickdoodles, which are also delicous!
